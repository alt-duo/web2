projects and achievements

i've been working on a lot of projects and have gotten some achievements, but these are the main ones (achievements may not be in a particular order)

georgebot

georgebot is a discord bot made in python. It can receive commands and do different things like moderation commands, some primitive a.i., and also random fun commands such as 8 ball and whatnot. This was probably my first serious real project, because I wanted to create a good experience for the users of the bot.

Aylus San Diego Branch Member

Aylus, also known as Alliance of Youth Leaders in the United States, is an organization that organizes and and attends different volunteer opportunities, aiming to spread goodwill to others. I really love volunteering at Aylus (most people say that for college apps, but I really do). Whenever I volunteer, I feel like its an impact to the world, whether it's helping low-income families by volunteering at the local food bank, or protecting wildlife by attending and organizing beach cleanups. Lastly, I teach kids how to do math (mainly beast academy) and english online, and also on-site (in person).

Because of my love for volunteering and my commitment, I've been awarded a Silver Presidential Volunteer Service Award for my efforts in 2023.

research intern at IEM UCSD

In the summer of 2023, I had the honor of interning in the Institute of Engineering in Medicine at UCSD under Dr. Linda Shi. In my internship, I did lab tests and researched about a new NAV 1.8 suppressor that is not yet in the market, doing tests to mice Dorsal Root Ganglion cells and also to PC-12 cells. This was an entirely new experience to me, and it widened my perspective in the medical field.
